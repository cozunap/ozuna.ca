<p><strong>Let’s Create Something Great Together</strong><br>
Hi there! I’m a graphic designer with 20+ years of experience crafting visuals for brands, print media, agencies, and digital platforms. I thrive on collaboration, and my favourite part of the job is turning ideas into designs that truly connect with people.</p>

<p><strong>What I Bring:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Mastery of Adobe tools (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, WordPress y CSS3) to create polished, versatile work.</li>
  <li>A deep understanding of print and digital design—from magazines and promotional materials to social campaigns and email marketing.</li>
  <li>A humble, team-first mindset: I listen closely, adapt quickly, and believe feedback makes every project stronger.</li>

<p><strong>My Approach:</strong><br>

<p>If you’re looking for a professional graphic designer who loves design (and is excited by well-chosen typography or an engaging layout!), let’s talk. I’m open to full-time, freelance, or contract positions where I can contribute, learn, and grow with a caring team.</p>

<p>Thanks for your time—I’d be honoured to help bring your company to the next level.</p>
